Here's How To Vote

1

When?

You can begin voting two weeks before the election day. Voting is open 9am to 7pm and ends at 7pm on election days.

2

Where?

Find your local voting place online! Locations are published three weeks before election day.

3

What?

Take your EasyVote card with you when voting. You don't need to take ID with you to vote!

4

How?

You are given two votes: a party vote and an electorate vote. You should research into each party and candidate before voting!
